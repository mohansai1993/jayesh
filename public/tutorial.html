How to integrate Adzviser with ChatGPT

Author: Zeyuan Gu, Founder & CEO of Adzviser

Updated: September, 2023

Prerequisites:

  1. Sign up on Adzviser
  2. ChatGPT Plus membership
  3. At least one workspace which has connected to data sources

Looking for Adzviser in the plugin store

  • Open ChatGPT and navigate to GPT-4. (screenshot)
  • Click on the “No plugin enabled” button below and scroll down to the plugin store. (screenshot)
  • Search for “Adzviser” in the plugin store and click install

  • Enter your adzviser login and password
  • Click to authorize
  • After you are being redirected to ChatGPT, you will see that Adzviser has been enabled as a plugin
  • Now you can embark on the journey of having the experience of reaching an understanding without engineering.
